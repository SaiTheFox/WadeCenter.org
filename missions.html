Missions & Volunteering

Providing mission teams opportunities to serve Bluefield, WV year-round.

Serving the Community

Our missions have a focus in relational ministry, serving at-risk children within our program and families in our community. Whether volunteers would like to focus on building relationships, engaging their talents, or just assisting where needed, we seek out those opportunities to cultivate a special connection to the community through selfless service.


Mission teams this past year have enjoyed leading our kids' program through children's worship, STEM activities, games, city clean-ups, serving in food banks, and other uplifting projects.

What to Expect

Extended Facility

The two-building campus includes several group gathering areas: a full-court gym, large cafeteria, playground, outdoor basketball courts, pavilion, and an auditorium with a stage. Teams enjoy watching movies, leading worship, or playing games on our large projectors.

Easy Registration

We offer flexible registration packages to accommodate for the needs of the team. Teams have the option to select our traditional registration packages, which provide lodging and meals, or a package with meals not included. Adjust your trip length as needed!

Local Activities

When teams are not serving, they enjoy a wide variety of activities in the area. These include: visiting the Pocahontas Exhibition Coal Mine, hiking local trails, viewing the sunset at the East River Mountain Overlook, or taking a trip back in time at The Corner Shop in Bramwell.
