Our Programs

After-School Program

Now Open!

The Wade Center's After-School Program is for students who are in grades 1st-5th, or ages 6-12, in the Bluefield, WV area. Students receive an approved USDA snack and dinner daily, in addition to tutoring, music lessons, art classes, children's devotionals, and other enrichment activities.


Students also have opportunities to partake in field trips and community service projects throughout the year.

Monday - Thursday
2:30 PM - 5:00 PM

Summer Camp

The Wade Center Summer Program is a seven-week day-camp for children in grades K-8 in the Bluefield area. Students participate in summer activities including Vacation Bible School, STEM projects, arts, sports camp, field trips, and much more!


Students are fed an approved USDA breakfast and lunch daily. The children are also joined by visiting mission teams who bring new and exciting aspects to our summer program.

June - August
Mon - Thurs | 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

*Closed the week of Independence Day

Volunteering Opportunities

Want to make a direct impact on the youth of Bluefield? We are always looking for passionate individuals to join our team.

If you wish to sign up as a volunteer, please come to our main office to pick up an application. (You must be 18 years or older to apply).
