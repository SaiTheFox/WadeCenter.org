Our Mission

The Wade Center exists to nurture Bluefield’s children, youth, and their families spiritually, physically, emotionally, and intellectually through practical programs and mentoring relationships.

"They will rebuild the ancient ruins and restore the places long devastated; they will renew the ruined cities that have been devastated for generations"

What We Offer

The Wade Center offers a variety of educational and enjoyable activities for everyone; including help with homework, hatching butterflies, learning 3D printing, art classes, and field trips.

How YOU Can Help

The Wade Center is all about our community. We do not charge families a fee for their children in grades 1-5 to attend the Wade Center's after-school and summer programs. Instead, we rely on churches, foundations, and local businesses and individuals to assist us in our mission. We also welcome volunteers to help tutors in the classrooms, lead devotionals, and pack weekend food bags.
